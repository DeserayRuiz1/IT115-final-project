Welcome to Northwest Hikes!

 Thank you for stopping by our page! My name is Deseray and I am the founder of Northwest Hikes. We are planning to bring you new photos of scenery, hikes and beautful nature shots from the Pacific Northwest area.

 I decided to showcase more photos of the Pacific Northwest nature, scenery and hikes. Lately, I am trying to find more time to hike. I really enjoy hiking with my daughter Lilly and my boyfriend, Andrew.

Featured Location:

 Richmond Beach Park is located in Shoreline, WA. This park offers a nice ocean view of the Puget Sound that is near by railroad tracks. The park has two restrooms, kid-friendly equipment and a large staircase for excerise purposes.
